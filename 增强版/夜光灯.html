Adjustable luminaire
日月夜光灯 

以日月为造型，利用咖啡渣材质的不透光性，制作一盏夜光灯。可通过旋转中间的圆形灯罩调节光线照射角度，月亮形状的档板用以折射灯光。夜间打开这盏夜光灯，可以按照需求调节灯光方向与亮度。
  Taking the sun and the moon as the shape, making use of the opacity of coffee grounds, a luminous lamp is made. The light irradiation angle can be adjusted by rotating the circular lampshade in the middle, and the moon shaped baffle plate is used to refract the light. Turn on the night light, you can adjust the light direction and brightness according to the demand.
